About Aetheria

The Flagship Cultural & Technical Extravaganza

Aetheria is the premier annual event of Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, transforming our campus into a vibrant hub of energy, creativity, and collaboration.

With a spectacular blend of music, dance, drama, and tech showcases, Aetheria celebrates the spirit of youth and the power of ideas. Our event brings together the brightest talents from across the nation for an unforgettable experience.

From electrifying performances to cutting-edge technology demonstrations, Aetheria offers a platform where creativity knows no bounds. Join us for three days of non-stop entertainment, inspiration, and celebration.
